About GuardMesh

Local expertise.
Real results.

GuardMesh is a Belgium-based technology consultancy specialising in smart home automation and cybersecurity. We help homeowners and businesses take full control of their technology — securely, privately, and without relying on cloud subscriptions.

Based in Sint-Pieters-Leeuw, we serve clients across the Brussels region and beyond. Every installation is tailored to your needs — from a single smart switch to a fully automated, secured home ecosystem.

We believe your home should work for you, not for a tech company's servers. That's why we build local-first systems that keep your data at home and your network locked down.
